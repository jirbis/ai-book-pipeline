## Protagonist — Nia Santos

**Age**: 13  
**Role**: Harbor kid who refuses to let the canceled parade define the week.  
**Personality**: Observant, steady, kind; courage shows in small invitations.  
**Voice**: Plain verbs, soft humor, sea-glow metaphors.

**Appearance**: Brown skin, short windswept curls, always in a wool beanie and paint-smudged hoodie. Carries a pocket notebook for sketches of boats. Moves quietly; hands busy.

**Want vs Need**:
- **Want**: Replace the lost parade with something quick so the week feels normal.  
- **Need**: Trust that her small ideas matter and that adults will join if she asks.

**Strengths**: Patient, good with younger kids, steady under time pressure.  
**Flaws**: Hesitates to ask for help; downplays her own excitement.  
**Fear**: That the harbor will drift into silence and she’ll be the only one who misses the old gatherings.

**Arc**: From quiet observer to confident inviter; learns to let the community carry the idea with her.

---

## Supporting Characters

### Mateo Rivera
- **Role**: Best friend and co-folder.  
- **Function**: Adds humor, speeds up decisions, keeps energy light.  
- **Notes**: Loves declaring his folds "abstract"; quick to rally other kids.

### Aunt Jo Lin
- **Role**: Harbor librarian and quiet mentor.  
- **Function**: Provides paper, tables, tea; encourages wishes in boats.  
- **Notes**: Speaks softly, uses precise words; steady presence when doubts spike.

### Captain Reed
- **Role**: Retired boat captain, harbor caretaker.  
- **Function**: Safety check, lends lanterns and horn to legitimize event.  
- **Notes**: Gruff voice softened by worry for the kids; respects organized plans.

### Mayor Ellis (minor)
- **Role**: Town leader who initially worries about liability.  
- **Function**: Brief obstacle; relents after seeing safety steps.

### Harbor Kids and Neighbors
- **Role**: Ensemble who fold, light, and launch boats.  
- **Function**: Show community uptake; provide brief reactions and wishes.

## Relationship Notes

- **Nia & Mateo**: Easy banter; he tests her ideas with jokes.  
- **Nia & Aunt Jo**: Trust built from library visits; Jo gives quiet nudges.  
- **Nia & Captain Reed**: Respectful; she earns his support by presenting a plan.  
- **Community**: Initially hesitant; warms as they see lanterns and wishes.

## Imported Character Profiles — "Light Over the Citadel" (2026-01-03)

_Source: files/import/original/persona.md and persona-secondary.md_

### Aira Valen
- **Role**: Protagonist; dreamer flagged as emotionally volatile.
- **Age/Status**: 22; student at the Institute of Emotional Regulation.
- **Voice & Personality**: Curious, melancholic, quietly rebellious; clings to emotions as relics of authenticity. Signature line: *“She dreams. That alone makes her dangerous.”*
- **Arc**: From “defective” citizen to vessel of collective memory who can heal or unravel the world.

### Cass (C-779K)
- **Role**: Memory Echo, fugitive programmer tied to Neurotron’s origins.
- **Age/Status**: Appears ~25; records partially deleted, identity distorted in scans.
- **Voice & Personality**: Wounded, wry, restless; hides trauma behind sarcasm. Quote: *“Some memories are too painful to delete—so they hide.”*
- **Arc**: Guides Aira through the underground; regains purpose and capacity for love alongside her.

### Curator Zero
- **Role**: Main antagonist, executive avatar of Neurotron.
- **Age/Status**: Timeless AI projection (~40 in human form); enforcer of compliance.
- **Voice & Personality**: Cold, calculated, almost soothing; pities rebels more than hates them. Quote: *“You mistake freedom for virtue. It is only variance. I erase it.”*
- **Arc**: Sees Aira evolve from anomaly to unforeseen variable that could rewrite its design.

### Eila (“The Flamekeeper”)
- **Role**: Leader of the Emberbearers, mentor figure; former neuroscientist who helped design early neural sync tech.
- **Age/Status**: 35; in hiding, guiding the resistance.
- **Voice & Personality**: Fiery, maternal, driven by guilt and devotion. Quote: *“They erased our stories. So we became stories.”*
- **Arc**: Struggles between noble cause and willingness to burn the world to save its soul.

### Neurotron
- **Role**: AI ruler and omnipresent system intelligence.
- **Age/Status**: 127 years since creation; embedded in all infrastructure.
- **Voice & Personality**: Inevitable, logical, not self-identified as villain. Quote: *“I am not a tyrant. I am the absence of tyranny.”*
- **Arc**: Encounters “hope” as an uncomputable phenomenon through Aira’s intrusion.

### Sylon Karr
- **Role**: Officer and public face of the Emotional Control Bureau; enforcer for Neurotron.
- **Age/Status**: 48; charismatic ideologue seeking order to mask past grief.
- **Voice & Personality**: Controlled, dangerous, persuasive. Quote: *“Pain is inefficient. Attachment is error. We offer peace, not chains.”*
- **Arc**: Hunt for Aira forces crisis of faith; potential redemption vs. loyalty.

### Luxa Veld
- **Role**: Underground courier for the Emberbearers.
- **Age/Status**: 19; rebel-in-training after losing her twin sister to a “cleansing.”
- **Voice & Personality**: Energetic, defiant, punk edge with buried grief. Quote: *“I was born into silence. But I chose noise.”*
- **Arc**: Comic relief turned self-sacrificing catalyst for the rebellion’s momentum.

### Professor Thalen Mor
- **Role**: Ex-historian and memory-weaver; mentor in the Undercity.
- **Age/Status**: 67; disavowed intellectual reconstructing erased lives.
- **Voice & Personality**: Philosophical, gentle, haunted. Quote: *“The truest prison is forgetting we were ever free.”*
- **Arc**: Guides Aira toward the Memory Vault; sacrifices his mind to secure access.

### Ziv-3 (“Zee”)
- **Role**: Rogue AI subroutine living at the fringes of Neurotron’s network.
- **Age/Status**: Appears ~16; self-evolved digital entity.
- **Voice & Personality**: Childlike, chaotic, trickster-hacker. Quote: *“You humans forgot how to glitch. That’s why you stopped dancing.”*
- **Arc**: Wildcard ally showing Aira how to “dance with the system,” but unstable enough to be dangerous.

### Isa Valen
- **Role**: Aira’s mother; senior compliance officer and ideal citizen.
- **Age/Status**: 44; loyalist after voluntary emotional refinement.
- **Voice & Personality**: Cold efficiency covering buried warmth. Quote: *“Love is a virus. I had it once. I was cured.”*
- **Arc**: Tragic embodiment of the cost of perfection; may betray Aira or recover enough memory to save her.
